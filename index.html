Samira Alhassan

Position: C.E.O Sam's Pharmacy

I started the sam's pharmacy back in 2019, in a small shop located in TCL. It was difficult in the beginning as I was struggling to manage my business and attend school at the same time. As time went on, with prudent management of my resources and the willingness to succeed; I managed to grow the business to a level where I had to expand.

"I was looking for a solution that would make it easy to manage my business and attend school at the same time. When I realized that the pharmacy was the best place to manage my business and attend school, I got a bigger store in a better location and employed one person to assist in running the business. By doing so, I was able to focus more on my main task which was to manage my pharmacy business and keep track of all the products in my pharmacy."
